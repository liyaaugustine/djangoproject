Library

Description

The Institute has a well equipped and spacious library. Almost all latest books on social work, psychology, literature, journalism, visual media and its related subjects are available in the library. Local and national dailies, periodicals, journals, magazines etc. are placed in the library for reading and reference. Above all, the students are motivated to make use of the library facilities to the maximum under the constant care of a well experienced librarian.

Timing

The library is open from 9. am to 5 pm in all working days including saturdays

General Rules

  • College Identity Card e sissued should be produced at the counter before entering the library.
  • Students are not permitted to enter the library with their Personal articles such as books, bags, umbrella, files etc.
  • Strict silence should be maintained in the library and its premise

Other Guidelines

Students are advised to use library during free hours.

Students can borrow books from the library on producing the Borrower-Card/Readers’ Ticket issued to them by the librarian. The Borrower- Card /Readers’ Ticket is not transferable.

The students are expected to use the books with utmost care. The borrower will be held responsible for any defect detected when the book is returned and he/ she will be required to replace defective book either with a new one or pay the compensation fixed by the librarian.

Students should enter the names and details in the prescribed register before entering the library.
