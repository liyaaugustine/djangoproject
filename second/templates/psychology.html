Welcome To Psychology Department

The department of Psychology were started in 2005 with B. Sc. Psychology course 20 students and 3 teachers. Dr. jacob Menon were the first Head of the Department of Psychology. In 2015 ST THOMAS launched M. Sc. Psychology course with 10 students.
